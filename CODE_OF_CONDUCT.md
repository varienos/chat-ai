# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a practical, respectful, and professional space for
contributors and users. Everyone participating in issues, pull requests,
discussions, or other project spaces is expected to communicate with courtesy
and focus on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but clarify when something is ambiguous.
- Keep feedback specific, actionable, and tied to the project.
- Respect different experience levels and time zones.
- Disclose conflicts of interest when relevant.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam, trolling, or repeated off-topic disruption.
- Attempts to obtain or expose secrets, credentials, or private customer data.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests,
or other contributions that violate this code of conduct. Maintainers may also
temporarily or permanently restrict participation when needed.
