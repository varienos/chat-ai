# FAQ / Sık Sorulan Sorular

> **Knowledge base template** — Replace this content with your most common
> customer questions and answers. FAQs are usually the highest-leverage
> entries in a knowledge base because they cover the long tail of repetitive
> questions your team would otherwise answer manually.

## Suggested structure

Group questions by theme. Use Q/A format so the assistant can match user
intent quickly.

## Example

### General

**Q: How quickly can you start a new project?**
A: We typically begin within 1–2 weeks of a signed agreement.

**Q: Do you work with international clients?**
A: Yes — async-first communication and English-speaking PMs on every team.

### Technical

**Q: Which platforms do you support?**
A: iOS, Android, web (responsive + PWA), and desktop (Electron when needed).

**Q: Do you handle hosting and DevOps?**
A: Optional — we can hand over to your team or manage it under a retainer.

### Commercial

**Q: How do payments work?**
A: 30% upfront, milestones billed monthly, 10% on final acceptance.

**Q: What about IP ownership?**
A: All custom code transfers to you on final payment.
