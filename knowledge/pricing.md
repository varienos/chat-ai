# Pricing / Fiyatlandırma

> **Knowledge base template** — Replace this content with your pricing
> information. The assistant will use it to answer "how much does X cost"
> questions and to qualify leads before handing off to your team.

## Suggested structure

Decide first whether you want the assistant to quote concrete numbers, give
ranges only, or always defer to a human conversation. The clearer this file,
the more useful the assistant.

## Example formats

### Tier-based pricing

| Tier        | Price        | Best for                          |
| ----------- | ------------ | --------------------------------- |
| Starter     | $X / month   | Small teams, single use case      |
| Growth      | $Y / month   | Multi-team rollouts, integrations |
| Enterprise  | Custom quote | Compliance, SLAs, dedicated infra |

### Project-based pricing

- **Discovery sprint:** fixed $X (1 week)
- **MVP build:** $Y–$Z range, depending on scope
- **Ongoing support:** monthly retainer from $W

### When to defer

If the question goes beyond what's documented here, instruct the assistant in
`system-prompt.md` to route the user to your sales team rather than guess.
