# References / Referanslar

> **Knowledge base template** — Replace this content with past clients,
> case studies, or testimonials you want the assistant to mention when asked
> "who else have you worked with?".

## Privacy reminder

Only include names, projects, or quotes that you have **explicit permission**
to share publicly. The assistant will surface this content to anyone who asks
— treat it like a public marketing page.

## Suggested structure

For each reference, include:

- **Client / project name** (or anonymized — "a Series-B fintech")
- **Industry / vertical**
- **What you built** (one sentence)
- **Outcome** (a measurable result if possible)
- **Optional:** a short quote or testimonial

## Example

### Acme Corp — Internal Operations Portal

- **Industry:** logistics
- **Built:** custom dashboard + workflow engine that replaced 4 legacy tools
- **Outcome:** dispatch time reduced by 38%; rolled out to 200+ users in 3 months
- **Quote:** "They moved faster than our previous vendor and stayed under budget." — VP Engineering

### Anonymous — Healthcare SaaS

- **Industry:** healthcare
- **Built:** HIPAA-compliant patient intake mobile app
- **Outcome:** 12,000 active patients in the first quarter post-launch
