# About / Hakkımızda

> **Knowledge base template** — Replace this content with information about
> your company: who you are, where you're based, how to reach you. The
> assistant uses this to answer "tell me about your company" and to provide
> contact details when a conversation should escalate to a human.

## Suggested fields

- **Company name & legal entity**
- **Founded** (year)
- **Location(s)** — primary office, time zones you operate in
- **Team size**
- **Specialties** — one or two sentences on what you're best at
- **Contact** — email, phone, social links
- **Hours** — when humans are reachable

## Example

**Example Company Inc.**

- **Founded:** 2016
- **Headquarters:** Berlin, Germany — distributed team across CET / EST / IST
- **Team:** ~25 engineers, designers, and PMs
- **What we're known for:** AI-augmented product engineering, fast MVP-to-scale delivery
- **Email:** hello@example.com
- **Phone:** +49 30 0000 0000
- **Hours:** Monday–Friday, 09:00–18:00 CET
